Frequently Asked Questions

What is this Quiz App about?

This app allows users to test their knowledge about blockchain through interactive quizzes.

How do I participate in a quiz?

Navigate to the Home page, select a quiz, and start answering questions.

Do I need to sign up?

Yes, you need to connect your MetaMask wallet to enter the quiz.

How many questions does the quiz have?

Currently, the quiz has a total of 10 questions.

Will the quiz have more questions?

Yes, the quiz will expand to around 50 questions in the near future.
