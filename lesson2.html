Lesson 2: Real-World Blockchain Applications

Supply Chain & Logistics

Blockchain improves transparency and traceability. Every step of a product’s journey is recorded, ensuring authenticity and reducing fraud.

Healthcare Records

Patient data can be shared securely among hospitals. Blockchain ensures tamper-proof storage and patient-controlled access.

Digital Identity

Blockchain can provide decentralized IDs, enabling people to prove identity without relying on a central authority.

Finance & Payments

Cryptocurrencies and decentralized finance (DeFi) allow peer-to-peer transactions and smart contract-driven financial services without intermediaries.

Voting & Governance

Blockchain-based voting ensures transparency, prevents tampering, and allows verifiable audits of results.

Challenges & Risks

  • Scalability issues with high transaction volumes
  • Energy consumption for Proof of Work networks
  • Smart contract vulnerabilities and bugs
  • Regulatory uncertainty in various countries

Getting Started

Explore public blockchains, try small DeFi apps, or experiment with testnets to understand blockchain applications practically.
